What a membership is — and what it isn't

It is a way to get more out of Luck.fyi: more Luck Points and faster earning, more saved number sets and history, more generator and SHA-256 entropy modes, results and crypto alerts, more Mini Draw plays, nicer shareable luck cards, discounts in the shop, priority support, and first access to new tools.

It is not a betting product, a lottery ticket, or a shortcut to winning. Lotteries are games of pure chance and every combination is equally likely — no membership, tier, or tool can change that. We don't sell predictions or an "edge," because none exists. Luck Points are an in-site reward currency with no cash value; they can't be withdrawn, exchanged for money, or used to buy real tickets.

Luck Points 🍀

How the points work

Points are the in-site reward currency that makes membership feel rewarding — and they have no cash value.

Earn
Daily use, Mini Draw, streaks & tier grants
Multiply
1.1×–2× by tier on everything you earn
Spend
Unlocks, cosmetics & rewards marketplace

Earning: you collect points by generating numbers, playing the daily Mini Draw, keeping a daily streak, and from your monthly tier grant — all boosted by your tier's earn multiplier.

Spending: redeem points for SHA-256 entropy unlocks, luck-card cosmetics, Mini Draw entries, and items in the rewards marketplace, including Lucky Shop discount codes.

The fine print that matters: Luck Points are not money. They can't be cashed out, transferred for value, or used to buy real lottery tickets, and they never change any lottery's odds. They exist purely to make the Luck.fyi experience more fun and rewarding.

Billing, plainly

How billing & cancellation work

During the beta, nothing is charged. You don't need to enter a card or any ID to try VIP features. The prices on this page are the planned launch rates, shown so you can see where things are heading.

At launch: monthly plans renew automatically each month and can be cancelled any time — you keep access until the end of the period you've paid for. Annual plans bill once a year. Lifetime founder seats are a single one-time payment with no renewal.

Cancelling: you'll manage or cancel your membership from your VIP dashboard in a couple of taps — no emails, no phone calls. Founding members keep their original rate for as long as the membership stays active.

Refunds: the launch refund window and terms will be shown at checkout and in the Terms of Service before any payment is ever taken.

Why trust Luck.fyi 🛡️

Fair by maths, honest by default

Provably fair, every tier. Numbers come from your device's cryptographically-secure generator (crypto.getRandomValues) with rejection sampling, so every combination is equally likely. Diamond members can download an audit certificate, but the randomness is identical at every level — we never sell an edge, because there isn't one.

Private by design. Generation runs entirely in your browser. Your numbers aren't sent to a server, and the core tools work with no account at all.

Transparent pricing. The price you see is the price you pay — no hidden fees, no surprise upsells, and founder rates locked for life. What Luck Points can and can't do is spelled out in full above.

Built in the open. The reasoning behind our tools and analysis is shared publicly on the Luck Crypto YouTube channel. We're an independent team — not a licensed broker, lottery operator, or gambling service.

FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

Does a paid membership improve my chances of winning?
No. Lotteries are pure chance and every combination is equally likely. Membership unlocks tools, points, cosmetics and perks — it never changes the odds, predicts draws, or sells an edge, because no edge exists.
What's the difference between Bronze, Silver, Gold and Diamond?
Each tier adds more Luck Points and earn multipliers, more generator and entropy unlocks, more saved sets and history, more alerts, more crypto tools, more Mini Draw plays, better cosmetics, larger shop discounts, faster support, and earlier access to new tools. The full comparison is in the table above.
Are you charging money right now?
No. Memberships are in beta preview — no payment, card, or ID is required yet. The prices shown are the planned launch rates, and founding members keep their early access and rate.
What are Luck Points and do they have cash value?
They're an in-site reward currency for generator unlocks, cosmetics, the Mini Draw and the rewards marketplace. They have no cash value, can't be withdrawn or exchanged for money, and can't be used to buy real lottery tickets.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Monthly plans cancel any time and run to the end of the paid period; annual and lifetime terms are shown at checkout. During the beta there's nothing to cancel or pay.
Is the Mini Draw gambling?
No. It uses Luck Points only — no money goes in and none comes out — so it isn't betting, wagering, or a lottery.
Do higher tiers get a secret winning system?
No. Every tier uses the same provably-fair randomness (crypto.getRandomValues with rejection sampling). Higher tiers get more features and convenience, never a better chance of winning.
Will my price change later?
Founding members keep their early rate for as long as the membership stays active. Public launch pricing may be higher.
