How the draw works

Double Color Ball in plain English

A Double Color Ball line picks 6 red numbers from 33 and 1 blue number from 16. Match everything for the jackpot — there are several smaller tiers below it too.

China's Double Color Ball (双色球) draws 6 red balls from 33 and 1 blue ball from 16. The top two prizes are pari-mutuel; prizes from 3rd down are fixed (¥3,000 / ¥200 / ¥10 / ¥5). Draws are Tuesday, Thursday and Sunday.

Total possible combinations
C(33,6) × 16 = 1,107,568 × 16 = 17,721,088

That single number is where every odds figure on this page comes from: you choose 6 main numbers from 33, then 1 from 16, giving 17,721,088 equally-likely outcomes. Exactly one of them is the jackpot line — which is why the jackpot odds are 1 in 17,721,088.

Heads-up

Why there's no fixed prize table

Double Color Ball is pari-mutuel: each prize tier shares a slice of ticket sales, split between everyone who matched that tier in that draw. So the odds below are exact and fixed, but the amounts change every single draw and can't be listed as fixed figures — anyone quoting you a guaranteed payout for these tiers is guessing.

Don't get fooled

Double Color Ball myths, busted

“Some numbers are ‘due’.”

Every Double Color Ball draw is independent. A number that hasn't appeared in months is exactly as likely as any other — the balls have no memory.

“Quick picks win more often.”

Random picks and hand-chosen lines have identical odds. Quick picks only win more in total because more people use them.

“Avoid obvious patterns.”

A line like 1-2-3-4-5 is exactly as likely as any other. The only reason to skip popular patterns is to avoid sharing a prize if they come up.

FAQ

Double Color Ball odds, answered

What are the odds of winning the Double Color Ball jackpot?

Exactly 1 in 17,721,088, computed directly from the game's number matrix — every line is equally likely.

What are the odds of winning any Double Color Ball prize?

About 1 in 16, counting every prize tier together.

Can a generator or system improve my Double Color Ball odds?

No. Every combination is equally likely on every draw. A generator just gives you a fair, unbiased random line — it can't change the maths.

Understanding Double Color Ball odds & your chances

Every official Double Color Ball draw is independently random and regulated. Each possible combination has exactly the same probability of being drawn, no matter how the numbers were chosen — birthdays, a generator, or last week's results.
